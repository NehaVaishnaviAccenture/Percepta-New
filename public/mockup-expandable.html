Understanding your GEO Score

The GEO Score is a weighted average of 5 AI-driven signals, each scored 0–100.

(Visibility×30%) + (Sentiment×20%) + (Prominence×20%) + (Citation×15%) + (Share of Voice×15%) = GEO Score
What each tier means
0–44
Fragmented
AI rarely surfaces you. Signals are too weak to appear consistently.
45–55
Emerging
You appear in AI responses but not reliably or prominently.
56–69
Competitive
Solid presence. AI mentions you but competitors still lead.
70–79
Leader
AI consistently features you. You're a top-3 recommendation.
80–100
Authority
Dominant AI presence. You're the primary brand AI recommends.
About the signals
Visibility
30% weight
How often AI surfaces your brand across queries in your category.
Sentiment
20% weight
How positively AI describes your brand when it mentions you.
Prominence
20% weight
How early and centrally AI places you in its responses.
Citation
15% weight
How often AI cites your brand as an authoritative source.
Share of Voice
15% weight
Your share of all brand mentions across AI responses.
